Afghan Cusine

Afghan culture has evolved over the centuries, and traditional cuisine plays one of the main roles in the life of this people. India and the countries of Central Asia had a great influence on the variety of the menu, but this did not stop the Afghans from surpassing their neighbors. Many dishes passed down from generation to generation, from the elders to the youngest. New delicacies appear every day.
